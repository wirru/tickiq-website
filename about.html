We're two friends who have loved watches since we were kids.

From our first Casio G-SHOCK to our latest mechanical acquisitions, we've always been fascinated by these tiny machines on our wrists. We appreciate everything that makes watches special: the artistry, the engineering, the heritage, the craftsmanship, and the self-expression they enable.

In an age of smartphones and smartwatches, mechanical timepieces are beautifully anachronistic. They remind us that some things are worth preserving not for their utility, but for their soul.

Time is the most precious asset we have. It's the one thing we can never get back, never store, never pause. Perhaps that's why we're drawn to these mechanical marvels that mark its passage with such grace and precision. Every tick is a reminder that this moment matters.

That's why we take our watches seriously. And that's why we wanted an app that could help us track and maintain our collections, ensuring each of our watches stays in peak condition. We built tickIQ to be the companion app we wished existed: professional-grade tools wrapped in a simple, beautiful interface.

Whether you have one cherished heirloom or a growing collection, we hope tickIQ helps you better understand and care for your watches. Because at the end of the day, it's not just about keeping time. It's about honoring the things that matter to us most.
